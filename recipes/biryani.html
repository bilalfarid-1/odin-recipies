Biryani

Description

Biryani, a South Asian treasure, layers fluffy basmati rice with savory meat, vibrant spices, and caramelized onions. This flavorful dish is a feast for the senses, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice – 2 cups1
  • Chicken – 1.5 lb/ ½ kg
  • Onion – 1 large (2 medium sized)
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Ginger garlic paste – 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Red chilli powder – 1 tsp
  • Biryani masala – 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala powder – 1 tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Coriander/ Cilantro– ½ cup
  • Mint – ½ cup
  • Curd – 2 tbsp
  • Oil – 3 tbsp
  • Ghee – 3 tbsp

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 20 minutes. I soaked rice in enough water. Drain the water and keep the rice aside.
  2. Now heat oil in a pan add whole spices when aromatic add sliced onions and green chilli. Saute well. When onions look sauteed add ginger garlic paste to it and saute until the raw smell goes. Now add chopped tomatoes, chopped mint and cilantro. Saute well.
  3. Now add chicken pieces, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, biryani masala and garam masala. Saute well.
  4. Now add curd and mix well. Now add 4 cups water and mix well. Allow it to cook with lid on in medium flame. This should take about 10 minutes.
  5. When the chicken looks cooked check for salt and make sure enough water is there. Now add soaked rice and give it a gentle stir. Now cover the pan with lid and cook for 5 minutes in high flame and next 15 minutes in low flame. When done put off the flame and fluff the rice gently and serve it hot with raita!
